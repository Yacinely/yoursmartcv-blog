If you apply online today, your CV is usually read by an ATS before a person sees it.

This guide shows you how to create an ATS friendly CV that works in any country.

## 1. Keep the layout simple

Use one column.  
Use clear headings.  
Use normal text.

## 2. Use the right keywords

Read the job description.  
Take the important skills and tools.  
Use them naturally in your CV.

## 3. Make your CV easy to read

Avoid too many graphics.  
Do not hide important text inside shapes.  
Keep your sections clear.
