Sending the same CV to every job is one of the main reasons candidates never get called.

Recruiters and ATS tools compare your CV with the job description. If your skills and language do not match what the company writes, you look less relevant.

## 1. Build a keyword map

Read the job description and highlight:

- the job title
- the skills
- the tools
- the important words

## 2. Compare your CV

Check if your CV shows:

- the same type of role
- the right skills
- the right tools
- the right language

## 3. Use AI carefully

AI can help you create a better draft faster.

But always:
- check that it is true
- fix the wording
- keep your real experience
