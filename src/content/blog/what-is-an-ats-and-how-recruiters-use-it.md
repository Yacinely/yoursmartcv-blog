If you apply for jobs online today, your CV often goes first into an ATS before a human sees it.

Understanding how an ATS works helps you understand why your CV needs clear structure and the right keywords.

## 1. What is an ATS?

An ATS is software companies use to manage applications.

It can:
- store CVs
- read information from documents
- help recruiters search candidates
- filter profiles

## 2. How recruiters use it

Recruiters often:
- search by keywords
- filter by experience or location
- review only a shortlist

## 3. Why this matters

If your CV:
- has bad structure
- hides text in graphics
- misses important keywords

then you may not appear strongly in search results.
